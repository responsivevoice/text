# @responsivevoice/text

## 2.0.0

First public release of the rebuilt ResponsiveVoice — a complete, TypeScript-first rewrite of the original library, now split into focused, independently-versioned packages.

`@responsivevoice/text` is the text-processing toolkit behind chunking and duration estimation, shared across the pipeline.

### Highlights

- `TextChunker` for intelligent, CJK-aware splitting of long text for synthesis
- Speech-duration estimation with currency and number expansion

Documentation: https://docs.responsivevoice.org
